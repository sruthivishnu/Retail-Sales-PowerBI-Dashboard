# 📊 Retail Sales & Customer Insights Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze retail sales performance, customer demographics, and product category trends. It enables users to explore business performance through interactive filters, KPIs, and visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Analyze customer demographics
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Track monthly sales trends
- Provide business insights for decision-making

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel / CSV

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Total Quantity Sold
- Average Order Value

### Interactive Filters
- Product Category
- Gender
- Age Group
- Date Range
- Clear Filters Button

### Visualizations
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Top Sales by Product Category
- Sales Distribution by Gender
- Sales Distribution by Age Group

---

## 💡 Business Insights

- Electronics generated the highest sales.
- Sales remained strong during the middle and final months of the year.
- Male and female customers contributed almost equally to total sales.
- Customers aged 26–35 and 46–55 contributed the highest revenue.
